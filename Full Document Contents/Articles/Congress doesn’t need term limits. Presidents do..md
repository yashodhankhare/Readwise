# Congress doesn’t need term limits. Presidents do.

## Metadata
- Author: [[Bloomberg Opinion]]
- Full Title: Congress doesn’t need term limits. Presidents do.
- Category: #articles
- Summary: The article argues that presidents, not members of Congress, need term limits to prevent abuses of power. It warns that limiting congressional terms could give more influence to unelected staff and lobbyists. The piece also highlights political challenges within the Republican Party and concerns about Trump's influence on U.S. and global politics.
